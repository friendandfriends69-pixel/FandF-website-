Quality Check

Nothing ships without passing QC.

Wholesale only works if what arrives matches what was promised. So every order — catalogue or custom-sourced — moves through the same four-step check before it's cleared for dispatch.

  1. 01

    Incoming Inspection

    Every batch is checked on arrival against spec, count, and condition.

  2. 02

    Sample Testing

    Randomised samples are pulled and tested for function, durability, and finish.

  3. 03

    Packaging Audit

    Packaging, labelling, and quantities are verified to match your order sheet.

  4. 04

    Certified Dispatch

    Only stock that clears all three steps is stamped, sealed, and dispatched.
